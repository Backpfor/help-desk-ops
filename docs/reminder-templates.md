# Reminder Templates

Envoyé après **3 jours** sans réponse du client. Si pas de retour → ticket clôturé.

---

## English (tous pays sauf DE et FR)

**Subject:** Reminder – Ticket [TICKET_ID] | Store [STORE] – [ISSUE_SUMMARY]

> Dear [Name / Team],
>
> This is a friendly reminder regarding ticket **[TICKET_ID]**.
>
> We have resolved the reported issue and are waiting for your confirmation that everything is working correctly on your side.
>
> Could you please provide us with feedback at your earliest convenience?
> If we do not receive a response within the next few days, we will proceed to close the ticket.
>
> Thank you and best regards,
> Help Desk – VI2VI
